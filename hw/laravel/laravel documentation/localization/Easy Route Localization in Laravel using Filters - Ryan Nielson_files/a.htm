                                RyanNielson Mod grrnikos • 9 months ago

                                Honestly, without looking at it too much, that may be your best bet if you want this kind of functionality. Here's an idea, similar to your's. Not tested though.

                                Maybe add an array to config/app.php like

                                $supported_locales = array('en', 'fr');


                                You could change the filter to something like:

                                Route::filter('localization', function() {
    $supportedLocales = Config::get('supported_locales', array());
    $locale = Route::input('lang');

    if (!in_array($locale, $supportedLocales))
        App::abort(404);

    App::setLocale($locale);
});
